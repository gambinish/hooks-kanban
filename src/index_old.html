Ho‘o Mahiki - Hop on over to Kaua‘i!

Getaway - Indulge - Adventure

Book two nights at the kama‘āina rate at any of these properties and receive 25% off activities below (15% at Anara Spa) and a complimentary pupu (made with locally grown Kaua‘i products) with purchase of two entrees at these restaurants. Look for the Ho‘o Mahiki badge from participating members. After booking your 2-night stay at a participating property, contact info@poipubeach.org with offer code: HOOMAHIKI and proof of a 2-night booking. Certificates for massage, dining, and activities will be emailed to you.
